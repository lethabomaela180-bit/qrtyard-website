About Us

"QRTYARD Café & Social Club is a creative container-based hub in the heart of Fourways — serving coffee, smoothies, and community spirit. Home to local brands, a barber, and lifestyle retailers, it’s a place built for connection and collaboration. We host weekly social runs, live DJ sessions, and pop-up events that bring people together. Whether you’re grabbing your post-run flat white, catching up with friends, or discovering something new, QRTYARD is your local caffeine dealer and community hangout. Support local. Stay curious. Drink better coffee."
